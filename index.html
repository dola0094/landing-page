Bob Marshall Wilderness in Montana
The Bob Marshall Wilderness just outside of Missoula, MT is one of the last places for remote adventure in the lower 48. It contains the most grizzlies outside of Alaska and you can go days without seeing another person. Click below to get more information on your next adventure.
